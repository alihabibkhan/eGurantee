Privacy Policy

eGuarantee System – Internal Use Only

Last updated: February 18, 2026

The eGuarantee System is an internal application used exclusively by authorized personnel for tracking loans approved or pending approval, forwarding them to the bank for review, and generating internal reports. No external users can access this system.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information strictly necessary for the system's core business purpose:

  • Loan application/reference numbers and status
  • Loan amounts, types, and related approval metadata
  • Internal user authentication identifiers (not shared externally)
  • System audit logs and access timestamps

We do not collect, store, or process any unnecessary personal data of loan applicants or any external individuals beyond what is required for internal tracking and reporting.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Managing and tracking loan approval workflow
  • Preparing and securely transmitting required data to the partner bank
  • Generating internal management and compliance reports
  • Maintaining system security and detecting unauthorized access

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share any data with third parties except:

  • Required loan data sent to the designated bank(s) solely for the purpose of credit review (transmitted through secure, approved channels only)

No data is shared for marketing, analytics, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to loan processing.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Strong authentication requirements
  • Regular security reviews and logging

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for operational, legal, and compliance purposes, in accordance with internal retention policies and applicable regulations.

6. Your Rights as an Internal User

If you are an authorized internal user and have questions about your account data or access logs, please contact the system administrator.

This Privacy Policy applies only to the eGuarantee internal system.
